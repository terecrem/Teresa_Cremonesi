Ubi
June 2019

Ubi is a service that help parents and children to live
a better experience during long car rides. Through this game,
the child can explore out of the window the view, and has
to find different objects in augmented reality. The parents
can control the game throught the application for smartphones
choosing duration, moment of beginning and end. In the end
all the object founded are collected in a collection of pictures
to live again the journey
