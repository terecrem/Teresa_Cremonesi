Cinanima

June 2018

The illustrations are characterized by the use of the same concept and the creation of different outputs. Hidden letters are combined into a general illustration realized with simple and geometric shapes. In the first case the illustration was created as a poster for "Cinanima International Animated Film Festival." In the second case, same experimental use of hidden letters, was the basis for the creation of an illustration for a library shop.

Polipo Magazine

April 2016

Polipo is an university magazine that covers different issues: current events, didactic and ordinary life. The style of the first issue is expressed throught the metaphor of sport connected to the main title "Get in the game". All the articles has full background illustrations representing different playgrounds. In the second issue different illustrations has the same graphic style and deals with various topics that are expressed in the single articles.

Playing Cards

December 2017

The main characteristic of the deck cards is the possibility of dual use. In the first case, it can be used in the traditional way, since all the cards are divided into four classical seeds which are represented by different elements of the beach. The number of illustrated elements correspond to the representative number of the card. In the second case, the deck can be used as a puzzle: all the cards, placed in the right position, in increasing order, create the main top view illustration.
