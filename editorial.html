DesignVerso is a magazines about Max Huber that wants to introduce the reader in the designer's world. The magazine is divided into four section that deals with different topics narrating his human and professional story. All the graphics and material choices are related to the language of Max Huber: his style is characerized by the overlapping of the four main colors CMYK. Consequently, the sections of the magazines are four and differentiated by the use of this colors. The covers of each section, realized through the print in relief, are characterized by the repetition of elements and signs typical of the designerâ€™s artifacts. In particular the cover of Brand Identity section wants to simulate the texture effect since the repetitive element is a logo designed for a textile brand.

Polipo Magazine
April 2016

Polipo is an university magazine that covers different issues: current events, didactic and ordinary life. The style of the first issue is expressed throught the metaphor of sport connected to the main title "Get in the game". All the articles has full background illustrations representing different playgrounds. In the second issue different illustrations has the same graphic style and deals with various topics that are expressed in the single articles.
