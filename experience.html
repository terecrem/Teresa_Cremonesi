Ubi Service

June 2019

Ubi is a service that helps parents and children to live a better experience during long car rides. Through a special telescope the child can listen a story in an interactive way, answering some questions and looking throught the filter. The main goal of the game is to find different objects in augmented reality exploring out of the window. The parents can control the game using the application for smartphones choosing duration, moment of beginning and end. All the objects founded are collected in an archive of pictures to experience again the journey.

Bu.

January 2018

By fear we mean an unpleasant emotion of strong concern or even terror caused by the presence of either real or imaginary dangers. This was the starting point for the creation of Bu: the whole concept is based on the prosgressive transition from a state of increasing fear to one final stage of unveiling that drastically polverizes the built up distress. The installation revolves around the display of five sequential 3D illustrations which, realized by overlapping several layers of paper in blacklit boxes, visually the story. The main scene is directly experienced inside a black box sorrounded by a dark presence evoked trought lights and sound.

Endangered Animals

January 2019

Development of a website game that wants to make people reflect about the deforestation of the Amazon forest. In the last years, the number of species in danger or threatened has increased because of human acts leading to what has been declared as a “global epidemic”. This project approaches the topic with a simple game: the user, using different tools, will discover some species in danger in the Amazon forest along with different characteristics of the animals.
